Harmful Reforestation

In the face of the climate crisis, many businesses and corporations are joining nonprofit groups and governments in planting trees.

But, when businesses promise to plant a tree for every purchase of a given product, they typically do so via nonprofit tree planting efforts, which, specifically in the tropics, tend to prioritize the livelihood needs of people over biodiversity or carbon storage.

Thus, only a few species of trees are planted and ultimately end in biodiversity loss.
